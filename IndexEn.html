Fultonvalue:

Fultonvalue, or the fitness factor as this value called, toghter with the seatrouts scale and fins you can see the health and fitness of the fish.

Fish with blank and loose scale, dark and whole fins with a value over 1.1 is generally good.

But should allways study how fat the fish is around abdomen before taking it home.

Some seatrouts can have a low value cause of the small tail part and other have a value over 1.1 even if its abdomen is sagging.

A bad fitness fish isnt that tasty and it fits better in the sea.

Release the fish gently without lift it up from the water and up on land.
